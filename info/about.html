About Me

Hello! I’m Emmanuel Agu-White, a multifaceted tech professional with a solid foundation in IT Support and a growing specialization in Data Engineering and Analysis. I’m passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights and building scalable solutions that empower decisions.

My journey in tech began right after high school, where I managed an internet café and honed my skills in troubleshooting, networking, and customer support. Later, I pursued a degree in Computer Science at Knutsford University College (affiliated with the University of Ghana), graduating in 2022 as the Best Graduating Student in Computer Science.

Over the years, I’ve expanded my expertise through hands-on roles and training programs, including the AWS re/Start Program (AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner) and the ALX Data Analysis Fellowship, where I mastered SQL, Power BI, and storytelling with data. In 2025, I joined the Amalitech Graduate Trainee Program specializing in Data Engineering, where I design and build robust data pipelines.

Beyond my professional work, I enjoy sharing knowledge through blogs, collaborating on open-source projects, and exploring creative ways data shapes everyday life. I like to think of my analytical mindset as a “superpower” — whether forecasting time without a clock or uncovering patterns others overlook.

My mission is simple: keep learning, keep building, and to create impactful solutions.
